[Postphoned]
Hotel Holiday

Hotel Holiday was supposed to be an event held during the end of December where houseless SJCC students would be given warm beds, good food, bath products and all-season clothing. Unfortunately, in order to avoid spreading COVID-19 through this event, we have to postpone until further notice. We are still planning on doing gift basket giveaways, so visit our Instagram for updates.

(ASG)
Student Community Online

At the beginning of my term with SJCC's Associated Student Government, there was very little student engagement on all of our channels. However, we worked together to create a marketing plan, host more giveaways, and attend Second Harvest Food Drives to make sure students knew we were there for them. Now, we have over 1,000 students RSVP-ing to our events!
