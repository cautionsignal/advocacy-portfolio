Why was this site created?

This site was created because there are hundreds of websites out there by individuals and organizations and corporations alike that compile resources, or sell the customer something, or publish articles and artwork to the internet. The only thing I have expertise in is myself, and whatever amount of wisdom I’ve accumulated along the way. There is only one of me, and that’s why the goal of this website is to clearly outline the projects I’m working on as I work on them, hopefully even years into the future, so I have a timeline and record of the past.

You're here because...

You want to know more about me! What a self-absorbant thing to say, am I right? But, if you learn more about my educational and career endeavors, you can learn from my mistakes. This website is something you wouldn’t find anywhere else simply because it’s about me as an individual. If you're an employer, you'll be able to access all of my work in one place, be it art or activism.

Supporting Local Black Businesses

Along with that, I'm planning on covering the cost of 125, $20 Nirvana Soul eGiftCards for students to enjoy some good coffee, become more aware of community businesses, and help keep this coffee shop open during the pandemic!

Hotel Holiday

Hotel Holiday was supposed to be an event held during the end of December where houseless SJCC students would be given warm beds, good food, bath products and all-season clothing. Unfortunately, in order to avoid spreading COVID-19 through this event, we have to postpone until further notice. We are still planning on doing gift basket giveaways, so visit our Instagram for updates.
