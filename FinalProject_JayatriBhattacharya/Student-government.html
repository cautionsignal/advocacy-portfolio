Recurring ASG Events

#Jags4Change: An initiative started by ASG to invoke community engagement by inviting students to volunteer with officers while feeding families with food insecurity.

    Mental Health Club: Started and prepared by Director of Student Engagement Caron-Klusendorf and organized by myself, this is a meeting for SJCC students to vent and learn about improving their mental health every Monday.

      ASG Regular Meeting: Every Friday, all the ASG officers meet to discuss how to improve student engagement, approve budgets, charter clubs, and respond to public comments.

Student Government

As Vice President of Associated Student Government at San Jose City College, I'm a representative of the student body. I strive to advocate for students on regional and statewide levels as well as organize campus events, activities, and programs.

      • ASG Events (I'm involved in)

      Christmas in the Park [FULL]
      Organized by myself and promoted by Chief Justice Seludo. The first hundred students that RSVP get a Christmas in the Park ticket for their entire family as well as a $20 Nirvana Soul eGiftCard.

        Hotel Holiday [POSTPONED]
        Organized by Director of Student Engagement; promotion and Clothing Drive organized by myself. The finances were managed by Student Trustee. This event was postponed due to the resurgence of COVID-19 cases, and because we're in Purple Tier. We hope to use our approved budget to create a gift basket giveaway of all the items we would've provided houseless students in January. This includes Bath & Body Works products, all-season jackets, and DoorDash gift cards.

Nirvana Soul
Supporting Local Black Businesses

Currently, I have a project in ASG where we purchase Christmas in the Park tickets for 100 SJCC students and their families. Along with that, I'm planning on covering the cost of 125, $20 Nirvana Soul eGiftCards for students to enjoy some good coffee, become more aware of community businesses, and help keep this coffee shop open during the pandemic!

[Postphoned]
Hotel Holiday

Hotel Holiday was supposed to be an event held during the end of December where houseless SJCC students would be given warm beds, good food, bath products and all-season clothing. Unfortunately, in order to avoid spreading COVID-19 through this event, we have to postpone until further notice. We are still planning on doing gift basket giveaways, so visit our Instagram for updates.
